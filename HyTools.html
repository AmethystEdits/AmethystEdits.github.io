HyTools By Amethyst

Explanation

To start HyTools, open console (opened with either F12, CTRL + SHIFT + I or right cicking anywhere on this page and clicking inspect then switching the view from "element" to "console") then type the following command:

startEngine(*"your name in hypixel"*,*"your api key"*,*"Your Profile name with proper capitilisation"*);

Example:

startEngine("PewDiePie","9gdb4wf3c-c95feb-4sdfv00-8adfhgd7-8cfsdfg21c7d9caf","Watermelon");

As long as you don't see any errors after that, the "N/A" boxes should now have values in them that match what is seen on hypixel. a kink of this is that the flippers accounts for your tax rate, so be mindful of that.

The numbers below that look like this are what is expected to be placed, as it is +0.1 coins above the current highest order. When asked to type your profile, it is exepected to be typed with proper capitilisation (Example: instead of typing watermelon type "Watermelon")

A "FullOrder" is a order of 71680, the max you can have in a buy order. "Seperate items" means items left over. for example, if you need to buy 2 orders of ender pearls, and 47639 in a "Seperate Order", you'd make in game 2 orders of 71680 and another order of 47639.

!!! IMPORTANT !!!

If profit is a negative number, it isn't profitable.

Ender Pearl Flipper

Explanation

The Ender Pearl Flipper works like this. You buy the selected amount of ender pearls listed below, and then with the assistance of a super compactor, claim the whole order bit by bit. When you have around 3 rows of inventory space filled with enchanted ender pearls, turn around, go to the adventurer NPC, and sell everything. (By the way this all should be done with nothing else in your inventory.) Rinse and repeat, its a great way to go from 50k ish all the way to 700k. After 700k its typically better and quicker to use the sub module below this one.

Gold Tooth Flipper

Explanation

The Gold Tooth Flipper works like this. You buy the ingredients to make a golden tooth (typically for around 50k per) and sell it at around 30% mark up (typically for around 70k). Here all you need to do is buy the gold, wether its enchanted or not (If its not enchanted its nice to have a super compactor with the enchanted gold recipe in there to not have to worry about that), then buy the wolf teeth, and craft them all together to make wolf teeth. This can be done extremely quickly for mad stonks, and is the whole reason this module exists. One thing to note is that you require the recipe for golden teeth unlocked, they're in the wolf slayer collection.
